Exploring



The world of Teyvat is one of Genshin Impact's biggest selling points. The vast open-world filled with monsters to fight, puzzles to solve, side quests to be invested in, and treasure chests to open. Not to mention the beautiful sights and scenery that the world of Teyvat provides. The world of Teyvat has 7 regions, (and possibly more) that you will be able to traverse through. As of the time of writing, there have only been three in its latest update; the three being Mondstadt, the City of Freedom; Liyue, the City of Contracts; and Inazuma, the Nation of Eternity. Exploration is surely a great activity for those completionists out there, who wish to have 100% exploration in every area of the game, wish to have seen every monster and creature, and have collected all the different local specialties in each region. Though exploring does not come so easy, as travelers will need to have strong parties in order to prevent being wiped out by monsters, strong minds in order to solve puzzles, and strong senses in order to observe and find anything that may be of interest.
